Central Triplex Meter

In the Central Triplex Meter table you can view information about the unique Central Triplex Meter of our System. The Central Triplex Meter contains information about the Real and the Reactive Power of each node of our System per day.

Fill in the following inputs with the Date and the NodeID that you are interested in. By clicking on each input a pop-up window will show up. Choose the values that you desire in each category and the corresponding diagram will appears.
